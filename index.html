Hampton Court Palace Festival

Experience global music superstars up close at the Palace

Enjoy the unique opportunity to get up close to the world's biggest music stars as they take to the Base Court stage within the stunning Tudor courtyard of Henry VIII's majestic residence.

Intimate world class performances

Over the last 28 years, an incredible roster of world-renowned artists have performed on our iconic Base Court stage, including Kylie, Bastille, Nile Rodgers & CHIC, Tom Jones, Lionel Richie, Gary Barlow, Paloma Faith, The Beach Boys, Liza Minnelli, Elton John, Eric Clapton, Andrea Bocelli, Bryan Adams, Katie Melua, Brian Wilson, Ringo Starr, Gladys Knight and Grace Jones.

Services

Luxury Picnics

Summer evenings in the Palace grounds. Make the most of your time in the glorious grounds of Hampton Court Palace by arriving early and starting your summer evening with a delicious ‘Best of British’ picnic or hamper, proudly brought to you by British Fine Foods. Relax in the gardens as you indulge in seasonal mouthwatering dishes from leading artisan producers from across the British Isles, all winners of the Great Taste Awards, World Cheese Awards or holders of a Royal Warrant.

The ultimate al fresco festival experience

Take your festival experience up a notch by adding a private Fountain-side Gazebo to your booking. Dotted around the baroque Great Fountain Gardens dating from the 17th century, they’re the perfect location to soak up the midsummer sun and catch up with family and friends whilst feasting on a festival picnic, sampling the street food offerings or tucking into your own delicious food.

Everything is better with champagne

Combine your Fountain-side Gazebo with a welcome bottle of chilled champagne to make your Hampton Court Palace Festival experience one to remember forever. Our dedicated hosts will be on hand throughout the evening to keep you topped up with refreshing drinks as the excitement builds ahead of the performance.

Base Court main stage

Located in the courtyard of Henry VIII's awe-inspiring original Tudor residence, walk in the footsteps of royalty as you journey through the palace before taking your seats in front of the intimate Base Court stage. Surrounded by stunning Baroque and Tudor architecture steeped in 500 years of history, this is your chance to spend an evening amongst the greats.

Garden Stage

Soak up the summer feeling in the East Front Garden as our emerging acoustic acts perform two thirty-minute sets from the Garden Stage whilst you enjoy sumptuous food and drink. You may even see future Base Court main stage stars performing before they hit the big time.

Step-free route

Hampton Court Palace is an historic building and, therefore, has uneven surfaces. There is however a step-free route from the Palace Gardens to Palace Courtyard please ask the stewards for assistance with this route. Within Base Court, there are six wheelchair spaces and six complimentary companion seats per concert on the wheelchair platform.

We offer a companion ticket for visitors with disabilities who require assistance. This is a complimentary ticket for a partner, accompanying friend or personal assistant (PA). Companion tickets, with exclusion of the wheelchair platform, are subject to availability.

Accessible parking

Please note that we offer complimentary blue badge parking which MUST be booked in advance of your visit as blue badge spaces are limited. Parking requests will close on midday on the day of your concert. After you have completed your purchase, you will be asked to input your blue badge number and your registration plate alongside your supporting documentation such as your blue badge. You will also need to present your blue badge upon arrival at the event. Accessible tickets can be purchased online.
